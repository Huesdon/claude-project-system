# Do Not Eat Yellow Snow

**Captured:** 2026-04-09
**Severity:** Medium

## Symptom
Snow with yellow discoloration is consumed, resulting in unpleasant taste or health consequences.

## Root cause
Yellow snow is typically contaminated with animal urine or other waste matter.

## Fix
Spit it out immediately. Hydrate with clean water.

## Prevention
Visually inspect snow before consumption. Avoid any snow that is not bright white. When in doubt, do not eat it.
